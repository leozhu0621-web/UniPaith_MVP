What changed & why

Design decisions

  • Nothing but the conversation — tracks, layer pills, progress %, strategy CTA, readiness + artifact rails all gone from the main view.
  • Warm opening: names the goal ("thrive, not just get in"), lowers stakes ("no right answers"), asks one easy question — not a list of suggested prompts.
  • Acknowledge → ask each turn; collaborative voice; one thing at a time (your spec's "warm curiosity").
  • Inline "✓ Noticed…" cards = earned progress + your spec's micro-confirmations, and editable (✎ tweak) so every inference stays the student's.
  • Quiet "✦ Your profile ▸" is the only chrome — opens the full living profile (next section).
  • The AI self-organizes across what used to be Profile/Goals/Needs — the student never picks a "track."

Open questions for you

  • Persona: warm-but-neutral "counselor," or give it a name/character?
  • Quick replies: keep "Not sure yet" / "You ask me instead" — anything else?
  • Voice intensity: the "lol / Ha —" casualness — dial up, keep, or more composed?
