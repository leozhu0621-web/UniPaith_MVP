### Section: FAQ

**For Students:**

1. **How is UniPaith different from Common App?**
Common App handles submission to ~1,100 member schools. UniPaith handles your entire journey — matching, readiness, applications, tracking, and offer comparison across any program worldwide.

2. **Is it really free for students?**
Yes. Institutions pay for their operations platform. Students pay nothing — no trial, no premium tier, no credit card.

3. **Does the AI make decisions for me?**
No. It recommends programs and shows you why. Every decision — where to apply, what to submit, which offer to accept — is yours.

**For Institutions:**

1. **Do we need to replace our current CRM?**
No. UniPaith works alongside Slate, Salesforce, or your existing system. It feeds structured data and AI insights into your workflow.

2. **How is this different from Element451 or EAB?**
Those are institution-side CRMs. UniPaith is a two-sided platform — it connects you to students with structured, standardized profiles, plus AI-assisted review and communication.

3. **What does pricing look like?**
Flexible plans based on institution size and volume. Free entry tier for eligible community colleges and regional institutions.
